Project Anamnesis: The Triage-to-Outcome Engine
The Goal: To replace the high-friction, 60-minute manual intake process with a 15-minute "Conversational Briefing" that ensures a perfect patient-provider match.

1. The Product Breakdown
A. The Patient Experience: "Sakinah Avatar"
Medium: A hyper-realistic, low-latency 3D avatar (Unity-based) using OpenAI Realtime API for sub-500ms voice response.
Empathetic Inquiry: Instead of "Select from dropdown," the avatar uses Reflective Listening. * AI: "I hear a bit of hesitation when you mention your work environment. Can we explore what specifically feels draining there?"
Goal Refinement: The AI acts as a "clarity coach," helping the user translate vague feelings into clinical goals (e.g., from "I'm stressed" to "I have high-functioning anxiety linked to performance-based self-worth").
B. The Provider Experience: The "Clinical Brief"
Before the human therapist ever meets the patient, they receive a pre-digested, structured summary in their EHR (Electronic Health Record):
The SOAP Note: Subjective report, Objective biometric data, Assessment of readiness, and proposed Plan/matching.
Biometric Subtext (TerraAPI): A heat map of the user's Heart Rate/HRV during the interview.
Example: "Patient's HR spiked 22% and HRV dropped during discussion of childhood residence, indicating significant autonomic arousal/trauma markers."
C. The "Killer Feature": Outcome-Based Matching
This is where the platform generates revenue. Instead of matching by "Geography" or "Insurance" alone, it matches by Statistical Success Rate.
The Logic: If the AI identifies "Complex PTSD" through the intake, it bypasses Generalists and matches the user with a provider whose Outcome Data (tracked over years) shows a 90%+ success rate with that specific profile.

2. Market Value & "The Real Use"
I. Eliminating "Intake Friction" (The ROI)
The Pain: Therapists spend 20-30% of their week on non-billable documentation.
The Gain: Project Anamnesis automates the Biopsychosocial Assessment. You aren't just "filling forms"; you're giving the therapist back 5 hours a week of billable time.
Scale: For a platform like BetterHelp, saving 5 hours for 30,000 therapists creates 150,000 hours of new capacity without hiring a single person.
II. Solving the "Churn" Crisis
The Pain: 20% of patients quit after session one because they "didn't click." This is a massive Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) loss.
The Gain: Because the AI has already "vetted" the patient's communication style and severity, the match is Data-Validated. High-fit matches reduce churn by an estimated 30-40%, exponentially increasing the Lifetime Value (LTV) of the user.
III. The Stigma Barrier (Truthfulness)
The Reality: 2026 data shows patients are more honest about "taboo" behaviors (substance use, intrusive thoughts) with a non-judgmental AI avatar during intake than with a human stranger.
The Gain: The human therapist gets the "Unfiltered Truth" on Day 1, allowing them to skip the "rapport-building fluff" and move straight to life-saving intervention.

3. The "Hippocratic AI" Technical Strategy
To be the "Hippocratic AI of Mental Health," you must implement a Constellation Architecture:
Primary Agent: A 400B+ parameter model (like Gemini or GPT-4o) trained on Bedside Manner and Empathy Inference.
Safety Supervisors: A cluster of 20+ "Specialist Models" (7B parameters each) that listen in parallel.
The Crisis Model: If it hears a suicide marker, it overrides the Primary Agent and triggers the Emergency Protocol.
The Clinical Model: If the Primary Agent makes a non-scientific claim, the Clinical Model flags it for correction.
Privacy Guardrail: Zero-retention data policy. The raw audio is deleted immediately after the "Clinical Brief" is generated for the therapist.
